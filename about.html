About Me

From medicine to YouTube to entrepreneurship - my journey to help people be more productive

Hi, I'm Ali

I'm a former doctor turned YouTuber, podcaster, and entrepreneur. After graduating from Cambridge University and working as a doctor in the NHS, I transitioned to creating content full-time about productivity, studying, and technology.

My YouTube channel has grown to over 2 million subscribers, where I share practical advice on how to study effectively, be more productive, and build a life you love.

I'm passionate about helping people work smarter, not harder, through evidence-based productivity techniques and systems.
